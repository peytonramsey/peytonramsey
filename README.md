# Hey, I’m Peyton Ramsey

I’m a data scientist who likes building practical systems at the intersection of ML, analytics, and applied AI.

I mostly work in Python, R, and SQL, and I spend a lot of time building ML workflows, RAG systems, and data products that I find improve my (and others) day-to-day life.

A few projects that reflect that:
- a compliance review platform built for a real insurance team
- a financial news RAG analyst for fast, sourced market research
- GymLog, a full-stack fitness app I built because I wanted one I’d actually use
- Kaggle comps to maintain my modeling skills and as a fun outlet if I need a break from a stressful project

A lot of what I build starts with a pretty simple question: why is this annoying, slow, noisy, or hard to trust, and how can I make it better?

I’m most interested in work where the output has to be useful in the real world, not just technical fluff.

Outside of work, I ran Division I cross country and track at UNH, and I’m usually into some combination of lifting, cycling, watching sports, and reading.
